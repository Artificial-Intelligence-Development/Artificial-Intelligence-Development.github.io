History of artificial intelligence

Norbert Wiener

Norbert Wiener theorized that intelligent behaviour was the result of feedback. That theorization influenced the development of early artificial intelligence.

John McCarthy

John McCarthy is regarded as the father of artificial intelligence. John McCarthy also coined the term Artificial Intellegence.

Artifial Intellegence Early Research Centers

In 1957 artificial intelligence Research centers began forming at the Carnegie Mellon University as well as the Massachusetts Institute of Technology.
